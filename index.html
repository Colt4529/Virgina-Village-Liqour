Our Story

A Denver Institution
Since 2000

Virginia Village Liquor has been a cornerstone of the East Denver community for over two decades. We're a neighborhood bottle shop in the truest sense — local, knowledgeable, and genuinely invested in helping you find exactly what you're looking for.

From everyday wines and craft beers to premium spirits and hard-to-find bottles, our shelves are stocked with intention. Can't find something? Just ask — we love a special order.
